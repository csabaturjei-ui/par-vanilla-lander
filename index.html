Post Apocalyptic Radio (PAR)

Your Decentralized, Social Music Frontier

Imagine a world where music isn't controlled by a few central platforms, but by the artists and communities who love it. That's the vision behind Post Apocalyptic Radio (P.A.R.) - a new kind of music streaming experience built for the decentralized future.

What makes PAR Unique?

Artist-Owned Music

Powered by IPFS. Instead of corporate servers, artists directly upload their music to a decentralized network. They own their work, control their content, and earn directly from fans - all without middlemen.

Solana Blockchain for Trust & Transparency

All music, payment info, and IPFS links are stored on a transparent blockchain. Artists upload a song, a record of it goes onto Solana, visible to everyone.

Social Discovery Ring

Discover music with a twist. Users recommend tracks to each other in a ring, creating a dynamic, community-driven playlist that evolves with every listen.

Community-Driven "Most Pinned" Radio

Users pin their favorite tracks. The most pinned songs play on the "Most Pinned" radio, automatically compiled - a playlist shaped by the community in real-time.

Future Incentives with a Custom Solana Token

While not fully implemented in this demo, this includes a future token system where artists and listeners are incentivized to participate.
